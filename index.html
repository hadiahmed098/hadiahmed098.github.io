About Me

I am an aspiring Computer Engineer studying at Purdue University who loves to work with others and give back. When I am given a task, I am driven to complete it to the highest of standards and surpass expectations. As a team member, I always contribute a variety of ideas to discussions. I am currently looking for an internship at a company that values a strong leader and communicator.

During high school, I lead a club that taught Python programming to middle schoolers. As an Eagle Scout, I volunteered in my community extensively and worked tirelessly to help others. I also was part of a team that developed software for the school administration.

At Purdue University, I am the lead for Software Saturdays, a Purdue initiative to teach other engineers programming and software tools. I design and create example programs, final projects, and learning material each week.

As a SubTeam Lead on the Purdue Aerial Robotics Team, I am responsible for coordinating with multiple teams and projects to create different technology stacks for controlling our autonomous UAV. Some of my tasks include designing autonomy algorithms, creating an image transmission pipeline, and constructing flight control software.

Outside of my education, I love to play around with photography and enjoy the outdoors. There is nothing better than a long hike through a national park armed with a camera. I also enjoy recreational archery and watching football.

Some of my technical strengths include Java, Python, C/C++, JavaScript, MATLAB, SystemVerilog, and microcontrollers/microprocessor.
